About A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

APJ Abdul Kalam the "Missile Man of India" was born on October 15. 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu He 
served as the 11th presedent of India and was a renowned secientist, known for his work in India`s space and missile programs

Scientific and technological contributions

* Missile development: As the chief architect of the Integrated Guided Missile Development Program (IGMDP), he oversaw the creation of missiles like the Agni and Prithvi, significantly enhancing India's defense capabilities.

* Nuclear program: He was a key coordinator for the Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998, establishing India as a nuclear power.

Leadership and vision :

1. People's President :- As the 11th President of India, he was known for engaging directly with citizens and was a strong advocate for scientific temper and education.

2. Vision 2020 :- He led a vision to transform India from a developing to a developed nation, focusing on technological advancement and societal progress.

3.Youth inspiration :- He was a powerful mentor and educator who inspired millions of young people to pursue careers in science and technology.
